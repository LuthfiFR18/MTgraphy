ABOUT US

Find out about our company and the founders of our company.

MATTHEW & THOMAS

Matthew and Thomas have been close friends since childhood. Since childhood, they have always been interested in the world of photography. They accumulated savings and bought their first camera at the age of 11. Since then, they are increasingly interested in the world of photography and decided to become photographers.

Starting as a hobby, then they finally decided to create a photography service company, called MTgraphy, where 'M' stands for matthew and 'T' stands for Thomas.

MTgraphy has been established since 2010, and has served more than 8000 customers from various countries, such as Indonesia, Singapore and Malaysia.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We sell photography services. We accept orders for photography with any theme depending on the customer's wishes. Customers can immediately do a photography session after ordering a predetermined schedule. We have a photography team of more than 1,000 people with each having at least 5 years of experience in the field of photography. We also have senior members who have been doing photography for more than 10 years.
